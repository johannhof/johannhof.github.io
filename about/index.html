Hey

I'm Johann. This is what I do:

I work as a full-stack software engineer and lead frontend developer at ZenMate, helping millions of people stay anonymous and secure on the web.

I organize Rust Berlin, a meetup group around the Rust Language. I have written some crates (pipeline.rs, difference.rs, markdown.rs, liquid-rust) and I'm co-author of the Rust static site generator Cobalt.

Most of my day-to-day programming is done in JS. I co-organize Nodeschool Berlin. I also published some npm modules. I'm a huge fan of functional reactive/declarative programming.

I'm a Mozilla AMO reviewer and contribute to Firefox and other Mozilla projects. I'm particularly interested in the Add-On ecosystem. You can track my contributions at my Bugzilla Profile.

At night I use my spider-like superpowers to fight monsters and evil maniacs.
